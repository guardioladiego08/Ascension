What health data Ascension reads

Ascension reads heart-rate data from Health Connect when you grant permission. The app uses workout start and end times to request heart-rate samples for the same time window.

How Ascension uses that data

Ascension uses heart-rate samples to render session charts, calculate workout summaries, and optionally store raw heart-rate samples alongside completed workouts in your account.

What Ascension does not do

Ascension does not require Health Connect access for core workout logging. If you do not grant permission, workouts still save without heart-rate enrichment.

Data sharing and deletion
